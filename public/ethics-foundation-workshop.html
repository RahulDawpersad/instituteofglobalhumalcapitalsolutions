Ethics Foundation Workshop

HR Management plays a critical role in the success of an organisation, and this role cannot be played successfully unless the practitioners behave ethically themselves and energetically drive and support the creation and sustenance of an ethical culture in the organisation. The building of an ethical culture is one of the key corporate governance outcomes of the King IV Code of Corporate Governance for South Africa.

Workshop Details

Agenda

This workshop covers key ethical principles, including the SABPP Code of Conduct, essential ethical competencies, and real-world insights through an Ethical Recruitment Case Study. Participants will also complete a post-workshop assignment to apply their learning effectively
